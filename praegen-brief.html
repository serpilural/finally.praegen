What we say — always
"German vocabulary for people living the language — not just studying it.
You've looked it up before. This time it stays."
ICP: 22-40, relocating to DE/AT/CH for work or study, A2-C1, English-speaking. The pain is retention, not ignorance — they look up the same word three times and it still doesn't stick. Prägen fixes this using the Ebbinghaus Forgetting Curve: the word comes back right before the brain drops it.

The Life

Why no product mention in posts
The brand earns before it asks
@praegen_app handle + bio carry the context. Posts that resonate earn trust. Posts that sell lose it. 90% of content has no CTA — only the Product pillar names Prägen directly. The ICP figures it out from the content itself.
Channel plan
Channel Frequency Output Tone Phase
Instagram Feed 3× / week Static image + carousel. HTML mockup per post. Brand voice — direct, slightly cheeky From week 1
Instagram Stories 5-7× / week Hero stories get full mockup. Lightweight ones built in-app. More casual, polls, questions From week 1
X / Twitter 3× / week Threads + single tweets. No mockup needed. Drier. More wit. Less warmth. From week 1
Reddit 1× / week Text post only. First-person founder voice. Honest, self-deprecating. Zero press-release. Week 5+ (4-week lurk first)
WhatsApp Channel 1× / week 5-7 lines. One word or situation. No images to start. Personal. Message from a person, not a brand. From week 3
4 decisions the founder needs to approve
German leads in Life + Word pillars
The ICP stops scrolling when they see a word from their real life. English is the punchline, not the hook. "abstimmen." is the post. The translation is the reward for reading.
Yellow (#ffd951) as primary post background
The most distinctive colour in the Prägen palette. No other language app uses this combination. Assigned to Word pillar — the most shareable content type.
Founder voice on Reddit (you, not the brand)
Reddit rejects brands. It welcomes founders. Posts are first-person, disclose the connection to Prägen naturally, and never lead with a feature. This is the highest-trust channel if done right.
4-week identity phase before any conversion ask
Weeks 1-4 build who Prägen is. No "download now." No pricing. The account earns trust before asking for anything. Full pillar rotation + CTAs from week 5 onward.
